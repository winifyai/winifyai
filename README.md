# WinifyAI – AI RFP Response Software for Public Sector Bids

[WinifyAI](https://www.winifyai.com/) helps companies respond to public sector RFPs, tenders, RFIs, and bid documents faster with AI.

Instead of manually reading long procurement documents, building compliance matrices, searching through past proposals, and rewriting the same answers across bids, WinifyAI lets teams upload their RFP documents, past responses, capability materials, and company knowledge to generate structured, grounded proposal drafts.

## Sample RFPs and Knowledge Hub Documents

This repository includes sample public sector bid documents and supporting Knowledge Hub files you can use to test WinifyAI.

### Folder structure

```text
samples/
|-- rfps/
|   |-- sample-public-sector-rfp.pdf
|
|-- knowledge-hub-docs/
|   |-- acme-capability-statement.pdf
|   |-- acme-past-performance-summary.pdf
|   |-- acme-service-overview.pdf
```

### Available samples

#### `/samples/rfps/`

* `sample-public-sector-rfp.pdf` – Example public sector RFP document for testing requirement extraction, response planning, and proposal drafting.

#### `/samples/knowledge-hub-docs/`

* `acme-capability-statement.pdf` – Example company capability statement.
* `acme-past-performance-summary.pdf` – Example past performance summary for proposal reuse.
* `acme-service-overview.pdf` – Example service description and company knowledge document.

These files can be uploaded into WinifyAI’s Knowledge Hub and used to generate RFP briefs, compliance matrices, proposal outlines, and draft responses.

## What WinifyAI Helps With

WinifyAI supports companies responding to public sector bids across the RFP response workflow:

* Upload and analyze RFPs, RFIs, tenders, and procurement documents
* Generate strategic RFP briefs
* Extract requirements into a compliance matrix
* Create structured proposal outlines
* Reuse approved content from past proposals
* Draft responses grounded in company knowledge
* Manage recurring RFI, RFP, and questionnaire-style responses
* Review, refine, and export proposal content
